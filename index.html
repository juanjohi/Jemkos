faq's

How do i clean Floors made of Mazeras Stones ?

No extra work is needed in cleaning Mazeras Stone floors just use a clean dry dust mop and water shall do the task

Do you do the installment of the MAZERAS ?

Yes we do laying of the mazeras at discussed prices with us. For both exterior and interior spaces

Where can mazeras be used ?

Mazeras can be used almost on any surface in both interior and exterior spaces from floors, fireplaces, bathrooms, walls, kitchen counters, walkways, garden landscaping and many more.

Why use Mazeras ?

Did we mention it gives a durable natural look as compared to the artificial products , and it being a Natural stone no worries about exposure to harmful chemicals
